FAQs

Exterior Coating Questions

Is this regular paint?

It is a professional exterior coating system approach. The final recommendation depends on your surfaces, preparation needs, product selection, and project goals.

What surfaces may qualify?

Many stucco, masonry, concrete, siding, trim, and selected exterior finish surfaces may qualify after inspection. Some surfaces may need repair or specialty preparation first.

How much does it cost?

Cost depends on home size, surface condition, preparation, repairs, product selection, access, stories, and architectural complexity. A consultation provides a project-specific estimate.

Is financing available?

Flexible financing options may be available for qualified homeowners. Availability, terms, and approvals depend on the financing provider.

What preparation is included?

Preparation is determined by the home. It may include cleaning, surface evaluation, repair recommendations, detail work, priming, masking, and application planning.

What products do you use?

Products are selected based on manufacturer specifications, the exterior surface, local exposure, and your project goals. The recommended system is reviewed during consultation.

What warranty applies?

Warranty terms depend on the selected products, manufacturer specifications, surface condition, preparation, and proper application. Applicable terms are reviewed before work begins.
